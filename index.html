01 — About

A decade of building quietly ambitious things.

Portrait · Studio
ÉL
EST. 2016

I'm Étienne — a designer, developer and creative director based between Paris and the internet.

For the past ten years I've partnered with founders, fashion houses and cultural institutions to build digital products that feel considered, considered, considered. My work sits at the intersection of editorial design, interaction engineering and brand strategy.

I believe the best interfaces are the ones you barely notice — they just feel right. My practice is rooted in typography, motion and an obsession with the last 5% of detail that separates good from unforgettable.

— Étienne
